Do you remember in the first Shrek movie when Lord Farquaad had the magical mirror from Snow White? After the mirror goes through his three royal options, Lord Farquaad turns to his team for guidance. And one very bright executioner looking fellow named Thelonious holds up two fingers and exclaims, "Pick number 3 my lord!". So while you may see two fingers, or even a sign of peace, I think of Shrek.
